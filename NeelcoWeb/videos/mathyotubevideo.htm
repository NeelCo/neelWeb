MathQuiz App

Click here for MathQuiz App Free Trial Download from Google Store.


This App is for every age group. Specially for early learners who can practice and become expertise in basic Math like Addition(+), Subtraction (-) and many more. 

This App is very helpful, kids who are in preschool and elementary can play this quiz and get more confidence in math.

How to Play Quiz:

Steps To Play:
Steps To Play:
Step 1: Click "Play Additions" Button.
Step 2: Choose number button and Click.
Step 3: Start Playing.
Step 4: Click "10+1" button to play in Timer Mode quiz.
Step 5: Setup Timer and start playing.
step 6: You can stop any time by clicking "STOP" button to Replay.
Step 7: Set the "Timer" again to start again.
Step 8: Press "Exit" to quit the Math Quiz.
